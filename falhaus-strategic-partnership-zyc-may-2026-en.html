02Quality and trust

The double-inspection system as a new shared baseline

The technical observations we shared with you are not the centre of this conversation, but the system that ZYC proposed to prevent them in the future is. That is the system we want to build upon.

Before moving forward, we would like to recognize something important: the industrial strength that ZYC has built over the years is the very foundation on which this partnership can exist. We know that behind every container that arrives in Argentina there is a factory with real capacity, with accumulated know-how, and with a team that masters the craft. Mr. Gao, that work, sustained over many years, is precisely what we valued when we chose ZYC as our partner, and what we trust we will continue to value, side by side.

The specific issues that arose (the pile foundation drawing, the difference between single-phase and three-phase electrical installation, the incompatibility between rock wool insulation and interior wall lining, and the quality of the folding module that we market as Falhaus Refugio) already have a path of resolution agreed with Nancy. We do not bring them back to the table to reopen them. We bring them in order to confirm the value of the new protocol that ZYC has proposed:

  • Double inspection before shipmentPrior written confirmation with Juampi on configuration, accessories, technical requirements and special customizations of every order.
  • 100% inspection of finished productsMaterials, appearance, electrical system, accessories and customized details, all reviewed before loading the container.
  • A reasonable compensation plan for open casesGoing beyond the cost of the missing item itself, taking into account the impact on Falhaus's credibility before its end customers in Argentina.
A concrete proposal: that these three elements be formalized as a permanent protocol of the Falhaus–ZYC relationship, and not as a one-time response to a particular case. That is what truly protects both of our companies in the long run.
03The value we bring

Argentina is won with a local voice and trust

We are aware that ZYC operates at an industrial scale many times larger than ours. We do not compete in size; we are different in that regard. But we offer something that ZYC cannot build from a distance, and that in Argentina matters more than scale: local presence, our own brand, and cultural translation in a market that buys from a familiar face.

Why trust matters more than price

The Argentine buyer, be it an end customer, a developer, a reseller or a provincial public official, buys from those they know. They decide after a conversation, after seeing the product in a nearby installation, after reading reviews in their own language, after feeling the support of a local brand that takes responsibility. The company that shows its face, that responds in their language, that understands the cultural code is the one that sells. The one that operates at a distance, does not.

Distrust toward Chinese products, and how it is dissolved

In Latin America there is a historical association between Chinese origin and a perception of low quality, sustained by decades of generic imported goods with no after-sales service. That perception cannot be changed with technical arguments at a distance. It is changed with local presence, with visible cases, with a Spanish-speaking voice that takes responsibility before the customer and that builds its own brand under its own name. Falhaus is that voice.

What Falhaus brings to ZYC is not scale. It is the cultural and commercial translation that turns a Chinese industrial product into a trusted proposition for the Argentine buyer. That is the piece ZYC needs and that cannot be built from afar.

04Foundations to grow together

Three conditions we hope to build together

In order for the local vehicle to operate at the pace that the Argentine and South American markets demand, we hope to be able to consolidate three economic and operational agreements with ZYC. We do not bring them as demands; we bring them as the infrastructure that, built together, allows this partnership to grow over time.

Theme Request Why it matters
Container financing A scheme in which Falhaus does not have to advance 100% of the value of the container before production and shipment. Frees working capital to sustain 10 simultaneous containers, prevents customs delays and accelerates commercial rotation.
Payment terms at 30 days Agreement on payment terms 30 days from container receipt, as a standard practice within the relationship. Generates the sustainable economic cycle that Falhaus needs in order to scale from 1–2 monthly containers to 4–6 without straining cash flow.
Centralization of inquiries through Falhaus That ZYC redirect to Falhaus every direct inquiry it receives from Argentina (buyers, distributors, contacts from trade fairs). Protects local operations, ensures quality control of the customer experience, and positions Falhaus as ZYC's single face in Argentina.
These three elements are the foundation upon which a long-term partnership is built. Without them, each company operates on its own. With them, both grow together under predictable conditions.
